About

This map shows flood extent detected from satellite radar during the July 2021 floods in central Switzerland, which caused severe damage along the Aare and Reuss rivers.

Drag the vertical bar left and right to compare the landscape before flooding (June 2021) with the detected flood area (July 2021).

Map legend

Detected flood extent Areas where radar backscatter dropped >3 dB, indicating surface water. Permanent water bodies are excluded.
No significant change Land surface with no detectable flooding between pre- and post-flood images.

Detection method

1
Pre-flood composite — Sentinel-1 VV backscatter, June 2021. Dry land reflects radar signal strongly.
2
Post-flood composite — July 12–22, 2021. Flooded surfaces scatter radar away from the sensor, appearing dark.
3
Change detection — Pixels with a drop >3 dB in VV backscatter are classified as flooded.
4
Refinement — Permanent water bodies masked using JRC Global Surface Water dataset.
