Blatten glacier collapse — Lötschental

A hanging glacier and large rock mass above Blatten village (Valais) collapsed, sending ice and rock debris across the valley floor. Sentinel-2 imagery documents the dramatic change from a green Alpine valley to a grey debris field.

How it works

Each map loads a pair of GeoTIFF rasters — processed in Google Earth Engine and served directly in the browser via georaster-layer-for-leaflet. No tile server required. Drag the swipe bar to compare before and after.

SAR maps use Sentinel-1 backscatter change detection (3 dB drop threshold). Optical maps use median-composited, cloud-masked Sentinel-2 reflectance scaled for display. Full methodology: METHODOLOGY.md.

Data & code
